# egyptian-real-estate-listings-eda
This project is dedicated to analyzing real estate listings in Egypt collected from PropertyFinder. This dataset included null values, inconsistent formatting, and incorrect data types, which required data clenaing before EDA.
As a result iof this analysis, I've discovered that real estate listings in Cairo, the North Coast, Red Sea, and Giza are priced significantly higher than average. Additionally, these regions are more likely to have a disproportionately high price-per-square-meter ratio.
At the same time, some regions, such as Sharqiya, Asyut, Al Daqahlya, and Aswan are more likely to have real estate listings priced below national average. This suggests that these regions are low in demand, and that may thus have worse than average quality of life.
The most highly priced real estate types in Egypt are palaces, villas, twin houses, whole buildings, and townhouses.
Moreover, in Egypt, real estate assets with maids present are more likely to be priced higher than average.
